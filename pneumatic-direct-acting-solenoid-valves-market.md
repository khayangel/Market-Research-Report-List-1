They come in different types such as three-way SV, four-way SV, five-way SV, and others. </p><p>A three-way SV valve has three ports and can control the flow of air between two separate pathways. A four-way SV valve has four ports and can control the flow of air between two pathways as well as block the airflow altogether. A five-way SV valve has five ports and can control the flow of air between three pathways, allowing for more complex air flow control. The "others" category includes valves with different port configurations or additional features.</p></p>
