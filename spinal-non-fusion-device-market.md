<p><p>The spinal non-fusion device market includes two main types: metal non-fusion devices and polymer non-fusion devices. Metal non-fusion devices are made from metallic materials such as titanium or stainless steel and are used to stabilize the spine without fusing the vertebrae together. On the other hand, polymer non-fusion devices are made from synthetic materials like PEEK (polyetheretherketone) or carbon fiber and provide stability and support to the spine without creating a fusion. Both metal and polymer non-fusion devices are used as alternatives to traditional spinal fusion surgeries.</p></p>
