<p><p>The Dynamic Distributed Ventilation System (DDVS) market includes two main types: Full Ventilation and Local Ventilation. Full Ventilation refers to a system that provides continuous air distribution and ventilation throughout an entire building or space. This type ensures uniform air quality and temperature control in all areas. On the other hand, Local Ventilation focuses on specific zones or rooms within a building, providing targeted air distribution and ventilation. This type allows for customized comfort and energy efficiency by delivering ventilation only where needed.</p></p>
