<p><p>Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) is a software system used to manage laboratory operations, data, and samples. On-premise LIMS refers to a system installed locally on the laboratory's own servers, allowing for greater control and customization but requiring higher upfront costs. Cloud-based LIMS, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers managed by a third-party provider, enabling easy accessibility, scalability, and lower initial investment. Both types aim to streamline laboratory workflows, enhance data management, and improve overall efficiency in scientific research and analysis.</p></p>
