It provides a convenient and easy-to-use alternative to traditional dosage forms such as tablets, capsules, or syrups. Several factors contribute to this growth, including the rising preference for non-invasive drug delivery methods, increasing consumer awareness about oral health, and the growing demand for over-the-counter (OTC) drugs and supplements.
