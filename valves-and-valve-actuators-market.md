Pressure independent valves and actuators ensure consistent flow regardless of pressure changes, enhancing energy efficiency. Butterfly valves and actuators are cost-effective and widely used in large-scale systems. Lastly, the "other" market includes specialty valves and actuators designed for unique applications, such as diaphragm valves for hygienic processes or solenoid valves for automation purposes.</p></p>
