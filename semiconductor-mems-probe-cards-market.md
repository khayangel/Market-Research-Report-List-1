<p><p>The semiconductor MEMS probe cards market includes two major types: vertical MEMS probe cards and cantilever MEMS probe cards. Vertical MEMS probe cards employ vertical probes to establish electrical connections between the semiconductor device and the test equipment. On the other hand, cantilever MEMS probe cards use horizontal cantilever beams to make contact with the semiconductor device. Both types of probe cards play a crucial role in the testing and validation of semiconductor wafers, enabling accurate and efficient electrical testing of integrated circuits.</p></p>
