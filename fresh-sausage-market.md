<p><p>Fresh sausage market types vary based on the meat preparation methods. Chopped meat sausages are made by finely mincing or dicing the meat along with other ingredients before stuffing into casings. These sausages have a coarser texture and visible meat chunks. On the other hand, ground meat sausages are prepared by grinding the meat and mixing it with flavors and spices before stuffing. These sausages have a smoother texture due to the homogeneity achieved through the grinding process. Both types offer different taste experiences and can be enjoyed in various recipes.</p></p>
