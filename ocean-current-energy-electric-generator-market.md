<p><ul><li>Horizontal Shaft Generator</li><li>Vertical Shaft Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ocean Current Energy Electric Generator market types include Horizontal Shaft Generator and Vertical Shaft Generator. Horizontal Shaft Generators are oriented parallel to the flow of ocean currents, allowing them to efficiently harness the kinetic energy of the moving water. Vertical Shaft Generators, on the other hand, are positioned perpendicular to the flow of ocean currents, enabling them to capture energy from the vertical movement of water caused by tides or waves. Both market types offer unique advantages in generating electricity from ocean currents.</p></p>
