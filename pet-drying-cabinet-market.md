<p><p>Pet drying cabinets are available in various types based on their fan systems. The Plain Fan Type features a basic fan that distributes warm air evenly. The Axial Type utilizes axial flow fans for efficient drying. The Turbine Type incorporates blower turbines with high airflow capacity. The Direct Exhaust Type expels warm air directly outside the cabinet. Lastly, the Hybrid Type combines different fan systems for optimized drying performance. These types offer pet owners a range of options to choose from based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
