<p><p>A Sample and Hold (S&H) amplifier is a type of electronic circuit that samples and holds an input signal at a constant voltage for a specified period of time. It is commonly used in applications that require the accurate capturing and processing of analog signals. The S&H amplifier consists of a switch that connects the input signal to a capacitor, which is then isolated by a buffer amplifier to prevent any discharge. This enables the analog signal to be sampled and then held at a constant value until the next sampling cycle.</p><p>The Sample and Hold Amplifiers Market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years.
