<p><p>Industrial control relays are classified based on their power capabilities into low power, medium power, and high power categories. Low power relays typically operate at voltages below 24VAC/DC and handle small loads. Medium power relays operate at voltages between 24-240VAC/DC and can handle moderate loads. High power relays operate at voltages above 240VAC/DC and are used to control heavy-duty machinery and equipment. Each type of relay is designed to meet specific power requirements and operational needs in industrial automation applications.</p></p>
