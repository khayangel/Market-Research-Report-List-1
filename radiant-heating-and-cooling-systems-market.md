<p><p>Radiant heating and cooling systems encompass different market types based on the medium used. Water media systems utilize water to transfer heat through pipes or tubes installed in floors, walls, or ceilings. Air media systems employ air to distribute warm or cool temperatures throughout a space, often using ducts. Electrical-based tubing systems utilize electrically conductive materials, such as cables or films, embedded in floors, walls, or ceilings to transfer heat. Each type of system offers distinct advantages and can be tailored to suit various building requirements and preferences.</p></p>
