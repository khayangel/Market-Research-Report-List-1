<p><p>Hermetic sliding doors are available in two main types: single leaf and double leaf. Single leaf hermetic sliding doors consist of one large door panel that slides horizontally to open or close the entrance. These doors are typically used in smaller spaces or where a single entrance is sufficient. On the other hand, double leaf hermetic sliding doors have two smaller door panels that slide horizontally, providing a wider entrance. These doors are commonly used in larger spaces or areas that require a wider opening for movement of people or equipment.</p></p>
