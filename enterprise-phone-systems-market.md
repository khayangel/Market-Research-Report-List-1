<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise phone systems can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based phone systems operate through the internet, allowing businesses to access calls and manage their phone system remotely. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as the service is hosted by a third-party provider. On the other hand, on-premises phone systems are physically installed and maintained on-site by the business itself. This option provides greater control and customization but requires more resources and maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs, balancing convenience and control.</p></p>
