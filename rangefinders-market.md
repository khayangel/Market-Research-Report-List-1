<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Rangefinder</li><li>Acoustic Rangefinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rangefinders are devices used to measure distances accurately. Two common types are photoelectric rangefinders and acoustic rangefinders. Photoelectric rangefinders use beams of light, usually lasers, to determine the distance. These are commonly used in surveying, forestry, and construction. Acoustic rangefinders, on the other hand, use sound waves to measure distances. They are often used in naval applications to calculate the distance to underwater objects. Both types of rangefinders provide reliable and precise distance measurements for various purposes.</p></p>
