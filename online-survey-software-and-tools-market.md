<p><p>Online survey software and tools are platforms that enable organizations to design, create, and distribute surveys via the Internet to collect information from a targeted audience. <p><p>Online survey software and tools are widely used for conducting surveys and collecting data. These tools can be categorized into two types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed and operated on the user's own servers, allowing for complete control and customization but requiring maintenance.
