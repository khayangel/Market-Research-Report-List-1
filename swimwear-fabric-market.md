<p><p>Swimwear fabric market consists of different types such as polyester, PBT or polybutylene terephthalate, and nylon. Polyester swimwear fabric is known for its durability, quick-drying properties, and resistance to chlorine. PBT or polybutylene terephthalate is a specialized polyester blend that offers superior stretch and recovery, making it ideal for active swimwear. Nylon swimwear fabric is lightweight, breathable, and offers excellent shape retention. These fabric types are widely used in the swimwear industry due to their desirable characteristics and ability to withstand the harsh conditions of swimming pools and saltwater.</p></p>
