<p><p>A shaftless home elevator, also known as a pneumatic vacuum elevator (PVE), is a type of elevator system that operates without the traditional shaft or pulley system. Instead, it uses pneumatic tubes and a vacuum system to move the elevator car up and down. This innovative design allows for easy installation in existing homes, as it does not require major remodeling or the construction of a shaft.</p><p>The shaftless home elevator market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years due to several factors. Firstly, the convenience and space-saving nature of these elevators have made them an attractive option for homeowners with limited space or mobility issues. Additionally, the ease of installation and relatively low maintenance requirements have further contributed to the market growth.</p><p>The increasing demand for home automation and smart technologies has also propelled the growth of the shaftless home elevator market. These elevators can be integrated with smart home systems, allowing users to control the elevator remotely and even monitor its operations for increased safety and convenience.</p><p>Furthermore, the aging population and rising prevalence of mobility challenges have created a significant market opportunity for shaftless home elevators.
