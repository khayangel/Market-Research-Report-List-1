<p><p>The commercial door closers market consists of three types: surface applied door closers, concealed door closers, and floor spring market. Surface applied door closers are visible and mounted on the door frame, controlling the door's closing action. Concealed door closers are hidden within the door or frame, providing a more aesthetically pleasing appearance. Floor springs are hydraulic devices installed beneath the floor, offering controlled closing and opening of heavy doors. These different types cater to various commercial door requirements, allowing for flexibility and customized solutions in door closure systems.</p></p>
