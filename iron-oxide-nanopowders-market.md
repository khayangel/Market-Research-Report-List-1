<p><p>Iron oxide nanopowders are categorized based on their particle size. Nanopowders sized between 20-50nm are commonly used in magnetic storage media and environmental remediation. 50-100nm nanopowders find applications in catalysts and sensors due to their enhanced reactivity. Nanopowders above 100nm are ideal for bulk applications like pigments and coatings due to their larger particle size.</p></p>
