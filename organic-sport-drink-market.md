<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The organic sport drink market consists of three main types: isotonic, hypotonic, and hypertonic. Isotonic sport drinks have a similar concentration of dissolved substances as the human body and can quickly rehydrate it. Hypotonic sport drinks have a lower concentration and are absorbed rapidly to prevent dehydration during shorter workouts. Hypertonic sport drinks have a higher concentration, providing a boost of energy and nutrients during intense workouts or competitions. These market types cater to different hydration needs and exercise intensities, offering organic alternatives to meet athletes' preferences and requirements.</p></p>
