<p><p>Campus recruitment software, notably categorized into two types, cloud-based and on-premise markets, is a critical tool for efficiently managing and automating campus recruitment processes. Cloud-based software is web-hosted, allowing users to access it remotely through the internet. This type offers greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as it does not require any hardware or infrastructure investment. In contrast, on-premise software is installed and operated on the organization's own servers, providing more control and data security but necessitating comprehensive hardware and IT resources.</p></p>
