<p><p>A mercury porosimeter is a scientific instrument used to measure the pore size distribution and structural characteristics of porous materials. It operates based on the principle of mercury intrusion, where mercury is injected into the material under increasing pressure. The amount of mercury that gets intruded into the pores is measured, and this data is used to determine the pore size distribution and other parameters.</p><p>The global mercury porosimeter market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Mercury Porosimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-pressure</li><li>Medium-pressure</li><li>High-pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mercury Porosimeter market is divided into three types based on pressure levels: Low-pressure, Medium-pressure, and High-pressure. In the Low-pressure market, porosimetry is conducted at relatively lower pressure levels, allowing for the measurement of larger pore sizes and macro porosity in materials. The Medium-pressure market involves porosimetry at intermediate pressure levels, enabling the analysis of smaller pores and meso porosity. Lastly, the High-pressure market uses higher pressure levels for porosimetry, facilitating the examination of even smaller pores and microporosity in materials.</p></p>
