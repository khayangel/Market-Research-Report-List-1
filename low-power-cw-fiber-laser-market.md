<p><ul><li>Single Mode Laser</li><li>Multimode Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low power continuous wave (CW) fiber laser market can be divided into two types: single mode laser and multimode laser. Single mode lasers operate with a single specific transverse mode, resulting in a narrow beam with high power density suitable for precision applications like micro-machining and laser marking. Multimode lasers, on the other hand, utilize multiple transverse modes, leading to a broader beam with lower power density but higher overall power output, making them useful for applications such as materials processing and welding.</p></p>
