<p><p>Sickle Cell Disease (SCD) is a genetic blood disorder that affects the red blood cells, causing them to become rigid and crescent-shaped instead of flexible and round. The abnormal shape of the red blood cells can lead to various complications such as chronic pain, infections, anemia, and damage to organs.</p><p>The treatment of SCD aims to manage and alleviate the symptoms, prevent complications, and improve the patient's quality of life. This includes a combination of medications, blood transfusions, pain management, and various supportive therapies. Hydroxyurea is currently the only disease-modifying drug approved by the FDA for the treatment of SCD, which helps in reducing the frequency of pain episodes.</p><p>The Sickle Cell Disease Treatments Market is projected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
