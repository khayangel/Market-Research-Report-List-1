<p><p>The machinery manufacturing market comprises two major segments: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. OEM refers to the production of machinery by the original manufacturer for sale to end-users. This involves designing, manufacturing, and marketing machinery directly to the consumers. Aftermarket, on the other hand, involves the sale of spare parts, accessories, and services for machinery, which are provided by third-party companies. Aftermarket serves to support and maintain the machinery already sold by the OEM, ensuring its smooth operation and longevity.</p></p>
