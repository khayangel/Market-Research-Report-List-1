<p><p>Technical fluids are classified into different market types based on their specific functions and applications. Dielectric fluids are used for insulation in electrical systems. Heat transfer fluids facilitate the transfer of heat in various industrial processes. Drilling fluids are used in oil and gas drilling operations to lubricate equipment and remove cuttings. Metalworking fluids are used in machining processes to cool and lubricate tools. Other types of technical fluids include hydraulic fluids, cleaning fluids, and automotive fluids, each serving a unique purpose in various industries.</p></p>
