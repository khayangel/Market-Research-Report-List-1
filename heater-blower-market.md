<p><p>The heater blower market consists of different types of blowers: down-blast discharge, horizontal discharge, and up-blast discharge. Down-blast discharge blowers are designed to blow heated air downwards. Horizontal discharge blowers, as the name suggests, blow air horizontally. On the other hand, up-blast discharge blowers are meant for blowing air upwards. Each type of blower caters to specific needs and preferences, providing different directions of airflow for heating purposes.</p></p>
