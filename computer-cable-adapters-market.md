<p><p>The computer cable adapters market consists of various types of adapters that enable the connection and compatibility between different cable types and computer devices. DVI-HDMI adapters allow the conversion between digital video interface and high-definition multimedia interface. Firewire adapters enable the connection between Firewire cables and computer ports. Gender changers convert the connection gender of cables. HDMI-to-VGA adapters allow the conversion between high-definition multimedia interface and video graphics array. Parallel adapters connect parallel cables to computer ports. SCSI adapters enable the connection between small computer system interface cables and computer devices. Serial adapters convert the connection between serial cables and computer ports. USB-to-USB adapters connect USB cables together. USB-to-VGA adapters allow the connection between USB cables and video graphics array.</p></p>
