<p><p>The thermal shock chambers market includes three main types: air to air thermal shock chambers, air to liquid thermal shock chambers, and liquid to liquid thermal shock chambers. Air to air thermal shock chambers expose products to alternating hot and cold air environments. Air to liquid thermal shock chambers subject products to alternating hot and cold air as well as liquid environments. Liquid to liquid thermal shock chambers immerse products in two different temperature-controlled liquid baths. These chambers are used to test the resistance and durability of products when exposed to extreme temperature changes.</p></p>
