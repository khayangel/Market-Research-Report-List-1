<p><ul><li>Vertical Machine</li><li>Horizontal Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bean milling machine market can be categorized into two types: vertical machines and horizontal machines. Vertical machines are designed with a vertical spindle that rotates the beans against a stationary, horizontal plate. This type of machine is known for its precision and ability to produce fine grinds. On the other hand, horizontal machines have a horizontal spindle that rotates the beans within a chamber. These machines are suitable for larger-scale operations and are capable of producing coarse grinds. Both machine types serve the purpose of milling beans for various applications in the coffee industry.</p></p>
