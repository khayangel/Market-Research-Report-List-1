<p><p>Emission Control Systems are used to reduce or eliminate harmful pollutants released by industrial processes or vehicles. The Electrostatic Precipitator is a type of system that uses electrically charged plates to attract and collect particulate matter. Absorbers utilize chemicals to capture gases and remove pollutants. Catalytic Reactors facilitate chemical reactions to convert harmful gases into less harmful substances. Catalytic Converters, commonly found in vehicles, use catalysts to convert exhaust gases into less harmful compounds before releasing them into the atmosphere. These systems play a vital role in reducing air pollution and promoting environmental sustainability.</p></p>
