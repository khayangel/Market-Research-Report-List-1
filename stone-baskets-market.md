<p><h1>Stone Baskets Market Size: Global Industry Overview, Market Segmentation and Forecast (2024 to 2031)</h1></p><p><strong>Stone Baskets Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>Stone baskets, also known as lithotripters or stone retrieval baskets, are medical devices used during minimally invasive procedures to remove stones from the urinary tract. They are typically made of a metallic basket-like structure with a mesh that allows the stone to be captured and retrieved.</p><p>The stone baskets market is witnessing significant growth due to the rising prevalence of kidney stones globally. Kidney stones affect millions of people, and minimally invasive procedures such as ureteroscopy and lithotripsy are preferred for stone removal due to their lesser complications and faster recovery time. These procedures require the use of stone retrieval baskets, driving the demand for such devices.</p><p>Advancements in technology have led to the development of more advanced stone baskets with improved efficiency and safety features, further boosting their adoption in the market. For instance, the introduction of laser lithotripsy, which uses laser energy to break down stones, has increased the usage of stone baskets for stone fragment retrieval.</p><p>The stone baskets market is also benefiting from the growing geriatric population worldwide. Elderly individuals are more prone to developing kidney stones, thereby increasing the demand for stone removal procedures and associated devices.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing a surge in demand due to the increasing adoption of minimally invasive surgeries and rising healthcare expenditure in developing economies. These factors, coupled with the growing awareness about minimally invasive treatment options, are expected to drive the market growth.</p><p>Overall, the stone baskets market is projected to experience substantial growth during the forecast period, with a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 15%. The increasing prevalence of kidney stones, advancements in technology, and an aging population are key factors contributing to this growth.</p></p>
