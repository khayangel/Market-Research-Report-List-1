<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The LCD sealant market consists of two types: Single-Component and Two-Component. Single-Component sealants are pre-mixed and ready to use, eliminating the need for additional additives or mixing. They are usually applied directly onto the LCD surface. On the other hand, Two-Component sealants require two separate components to be mixed together before application. This type offers more flexibility in terms of customization and stronger bonding properties. Both types are widely used in the LCD manufacturing industry, providing protection and insulation for LCD screens.</p></p>
