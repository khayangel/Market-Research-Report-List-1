<p><p>The Advanced Process Control (APC) market consists of various types of control systems that aim to optimize industrial processes. The Advanced Regulatory Control focuses on maintaining process variables within desired limits. Multivariable Model Predictive Control utilizes mathematical models to make predictions and optimize multiple variables simultaneously. Inferential Control estimates the value of inaccessible variables based on available information. Sequential Control coordinates the sequence of actions required in a process. Compressor Control specifically focuses on optimizing the operation of compressors in industries. These control types offer efficient and effective process optimization solutions.</p></p>
