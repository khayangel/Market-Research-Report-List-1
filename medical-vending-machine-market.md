<p><p>A medical vending machine is an automated retail machine that dispenses medical supplies, over-the-counter medications, healthcare products, and personal protective equipment (PPE). One of the main factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for self-service technologies in the healthcare industry. Medical vending machines provide an efficient way for healthcare providers and patients to access necessary medical supplies without the need for human intervention.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has also boosted the demand for medical vending machines. These machines can be used to dispense PPE such as masks, gloves, sanitizers, and disinfectants, which have become essential in preventing the spread of the virus. Furthermore, the adoption of RFID technology in medical vending machines enables better inventory management and reduces the risk of stock-out situations, further fueling market growth.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends. For instance, the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms in medical vending machines allows for better inventory management, personalized recommendations, and predictive maintenance.
