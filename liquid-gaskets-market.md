<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Liquid Gaskets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Formed-In-Place (FIP) Type</li><li>Cured-In-Place (CIP) Type</li><li>Injected-In-Place (IIP) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid gaskets are used in various industries for sealing applications. Formed-In-Place (FIP) type gaskets are applied as a liquid that forms a solid seal upon curing. Cured-In-Place (CIP) gaskets are pre-formed and applied as a liquid, solidifying through curing. Injected-In-Place (IIP) gaskets are injected into gaps or crevices, resulting in a solid seal after curing. These gasket types offer flexibility, ease of application, and effective sealing characteristics, catering to different sealing requirements across industries while ensuring efficient and reliable sealing performance.</p></p>
