<p><p>ESG reporting and consultancy refer to the provision of services related to Environmental, Social, and Governance factors by firms specializing in sustainable investing and corporate responsibility. It helps organizations identify opportunities to enhance their ESG performance, mitigate risks, and align with global sustainability goals.</p><p>The ESG reporting and consultancy market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing recognition of the importance of sustainable and responsible practices by investors, regulators, and consumers has led companies to prioritize ESG reporting and consultancy services. For example, the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the Paris Agreement have urged businesses to take action, leading to a surge in demand for ESG services.
