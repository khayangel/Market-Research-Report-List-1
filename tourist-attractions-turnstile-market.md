<p><p>The tourist attractions turnstile market consists of two main types: half height and full height turnstiles. Half height turnstiles are waist-height gates that provide easy access for visitors at places like museums or amusement parks. They are designed to control entry flow and ensure ticket validation. On the other hand, full height turnstiles are taller and more secure, commonly used in places with higher security needs, like stadiums or train stations, preventing unauthorized access and allowing only one person to pass at a time.</p></p>
