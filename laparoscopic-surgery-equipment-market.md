<p><ul><li>Rigid Laparoscope</li><li>Flexible Laparoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laparoscopic surgery equipment market includes two main types of instruments: rigid laparoscope and flexible laparoscopy. Rigid laparoscopes are long, straight, and have a fixed position. They are commonly used in procedures that require a direct view of the surgical site. On the other hand, flexible laparoscopy includes instruments with flexible tips, allowing for better access to hard-to-reach areas. This type of equipment is often utilized in procedures that involve complex anatomical structures or require a more flexible approach.</p></p>
