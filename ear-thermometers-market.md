<p><p>Ear thermometers are used to measure body temperature in a convenient and hygienic way. Non-contact type ear thermometers use infrared technology to detect temperature from a distance, making them ideal for children or individuals who may be uncomfortable with direct contact. Contact type ear thermometers require insertion into the ear canal for accurate measurements and are commonly used in healthcare settings. Both types offer fast and reliable temperature readings, with the non-contact type being more suitable for quick and hassle-free measurements, while the contact type offers increased precision and is commonly used by professionals.</p></p>
