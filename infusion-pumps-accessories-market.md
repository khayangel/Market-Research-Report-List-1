<p><p>Infusion pumps and accessories are medical devices used to deliver fluids, such as medications or nutrients, into a patient's body in a controlled manner. Factors driving this market growth include the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, increasing geriatric population, technological advancements in infusion pumps, and the growing demand for home infusion therapies.</p><p>Market growth analysis suggests that the market is expected to expand at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.6% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several trends in the industry.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the infusion pumps and accessories market is the development of smart infusion pumps. These pumps are equipped with advanced features such as dose error reduction systems, wireless connectivity, and electronic medical records integration. Smart infusion pumps help in reducing medication errors, improving patient safety, and enhancing workflow efficiency for healthcare providers.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing adoption of ambulatory infusion pumps. These portable pumps allow patients to receive continuous medication infusion while maintaining their normal daily activities. Ambulatory infusion pumps are especially beneficial for patients requiring long-term therapy or those who prefer to receive treatment at home rather than in a healthcare facility.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a shift towards infusion pump systems that offer integration with electronic health record (EHR) systems. This integration enables seamless flow of patient information, reduces documentation errors, and enhances patient care quality.</p><p>Overall, the infusion pumps and accessories market is expected to grow steadily due to the increasing demand for efficient drug delivery systems and advancements in technology.
