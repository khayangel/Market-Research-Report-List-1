<p><p>The OTC orthopedic braces market is divided into different types, including soft and elastic braces and supports, hinged braces and supports, and hard and rigid braces and supports. Soft and elastic braces are made from flexible materials like neoprene and provide compression and support. Hinged braces have adjustable hinges that offer stability and restrict movement. Hard and rigid braces are constructed from sturdy materials such as plastic or metal and are designed to immobilize and protect the injured area. These different types cater to various orthopedic needs and provide different levels of support and protection.</p></p>
