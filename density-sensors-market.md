<p><p>Density sensors are used to measure the mass per unit volume of a substance. They can be classified into three market types based on their mounting positions. Mounted to top density sensors are typically installed on the upper part of a container or vessel to measure the density of the liquid or gas inside. On the other hand, mounted to side density sensors are positioned on the walls of the container or vessel for density measurement. Lastly, the "Others" market includes density sensors that utilize different mounting positions or innovative techniques to accurately determine density.</p></p>
