<p><p>Modular ice makers are refrigeration units used to produce ice in commercial settings. There are two main types of modular ice makers: water-cooled and air-cooled. Water-cooled ice makers use water to cool the condenser, which results in efficient ice production but requires a constant water supply. On the other hand, air-cooled ice makers use air to cool the condenser and do not require a water supply, making them more cost-effective and easier to install. Both types have their advantages and are suitable for various applications in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
