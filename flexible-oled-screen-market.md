<p><p>The flexible OLED screen market is divided into two main types: glass and flexible/rigid plastic. Glass-based OLED screens are made using traditional glass materials, making them more rigid but also more fragile. On the other hand, flexible/rigid plastic OLED screens are made using plastic materials, allowing them to be more bendable and resistant to impact. Both types have their advantages and applications. The glass OLED screens are typically used in devices that prioritize visual clarity and durability, while the flexible/rigid plastic screens are suitable for devices that require flexibility and durability, such as foldable smartphones.</p></p>
