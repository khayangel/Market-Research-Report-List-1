<p><p>The stackable USB connector market encompasses three main types: USB Type A, USB Type B, and USB Type C. USB Type A is the most common and traditional connector found on computers and hubs. USB Type B connectors are mostly used in peripheral devices such as printers. USB Type C is a newer and versatile connector that can be used for data transfer, video output, and charging. Its small design and reversible nature make it ideal for mobile devices and laptops. These three types cater to different connectivity needs, with USB Type C gaining popularity due to its advanced features.</p></p>
