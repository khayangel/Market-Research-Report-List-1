<p><p>Continuous welded pipe and tube market consists of two main types: straight seam welded pipe and helical welded pipe. Straight seam welded pipe is produced by welding a long strip of steel in a straight line, while helical welded pipe is formed by spirally welding steel strips. Both types are used in various industries such as oil and gas, construction, and automotive for their high strength, durability, and cost-effectiveness in transporting fluids and gases.</p></p>
