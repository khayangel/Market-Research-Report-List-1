<p><strong>The Datacenter Servers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tower Server</li><li>Rack Server</li><li>Blade Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Datacenter servers come in various types to cater to different needs. Tower servers are standalone units resembling desktop computers, suitable for small businesses or remote offices. Rack servers are designed to be stacked in server racks, saving space and facilitating easier management. They are commonly used in medium to large datacenters. Blade servers are compact units that slide into blade enclosures, allowing for high density and scalability. They are commonly used in large datacenters where power and space efficiency are paramount.</p></p>
