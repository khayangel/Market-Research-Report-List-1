<p><p>The high throughput pipetting market consists of two main types: manual pipetting systems and electric pipetting systems. Manual pipetting systems require manual operation and are commonly used in laboratories where low to medium throughput is required. Electric pipetting systems, on the other hand, are automated and offer higher throughput capabilities by eliminating the need for manual pipetting. These systems are often used in high-throughput screening applications, where large quantities of samples need to be processed quickly and accurately.</p></p>
