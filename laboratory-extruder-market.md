<p><strong>The Laboratory Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Laboratory Extruder</li><li>Twin Screw Laboratory Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Extruder Market includes two types of extruders - Single Screw and Twin Screw. A Single Screw Laboratory Extruder is designed with a single rotating screw that pushes the material through the extrusion barrel, generating heat and pressure to melt and form the material. On the other hand, Twin Screw Laboratory Extruders have two intermeshing screws that work together to process the material. Twin screw extruders offer better mixing and handling of a wider range of materials compared to single screw extruders.</p></p>
