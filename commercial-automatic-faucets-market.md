<p><p>Commercial automatic faucets can be classified into two main types: DC power and AC power. DC power refers to faucets that are operated by direct current electricity, typically provided by batteries or solar panels. These faucets are ideal for areas with limited or no access to electrical power sources. On the other hand, AC power faucets are powered by alternating current electricity, which is readily available in commercial buildings. These faucets typically require connection to an electrical outlet and are commonly found in areas with reliable access to power.</p></p>
