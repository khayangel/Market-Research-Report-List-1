<p><p>The Mechatronic Lock market can be divided into two types: contact type and contactless type. The contact type locks require physical contact to operate, such as a key or keypad input. These locks are commonly found in traditional mechanical lock systems. On the other hand, the contactless type locks do not require physical contact and use technologies like RFID or biometrics to operate. These locks are more advanced and offer convenience and increased security.</p></p>
