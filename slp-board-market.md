<p><ul><li>Less Than 15/15µm Line Width/Line Spacing</li><li>15/15µm-30µm Line Width/Line Spacing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SLP (single-layer piezoelectric) board market can be categorized into two types based on line width and line spacing. The first type is for boards with line width and line spacing of less than 15/15µm. These boards are designed for applications that require very fine circuitry, such as high-density electronics and miniature devices. The second type is for boards with line width and line spacing ranging from 15/15µm to 30µm. These boards are suitable for applications where moderate circuitry density is needed, such as in consumer electronics and automotive devices.</p></p>
