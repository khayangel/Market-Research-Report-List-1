<p><p>Quartz resonators are electronic components used in various applications for accurate timing and frequency control. The market for quartz resonators is divided into two major types: DIP (Dual Inline Package) and SMD (Surface Mount Device). DIP resonators are larger in size and have two rows of pins for connection, making them suitable for through-hole mounting on circuit boards. On the other hand, SMD resonators are compact and designed for surface mounting directly on the PCB. The choice between DIP and SMD resonators depends on the specific requirements and design of the electronic circuit.</p></p>
