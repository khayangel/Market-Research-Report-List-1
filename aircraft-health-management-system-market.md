<p><p>Aircraft Health Management System (AHMS), also known as Aircraft Condition Monitoring System (ACMS), is a comprehensive software-based technology that is used to monitor the real-time health and performance of aircraft systems. It collects data from various sensors installed on the aircraft and analyzes it to detect any anomalies or potential faults. AHMS helps in predicting and preventing future failures, optimizing maintenance schedules, reducing unscheduled maintenance, and improving overall operational efficiency of the aircraft.</p><p>The Aircraft Health Management System market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. Factors such as increasing aviation safety regulations, growing demand for real-time data analysis, and the need for reduction in maintenance costs are driving the adoption of AHMS in the aviation industry.
