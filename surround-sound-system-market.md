Dolby Digital introduces more advanced compression technology for enhanced audio quality and is widely used in home theaters. Similarly, DTS (Digital Theater Systems) provides high-quality surround sound through its encoding techniques, making it a competitor to Dolby Digital in the market.</p></p>
