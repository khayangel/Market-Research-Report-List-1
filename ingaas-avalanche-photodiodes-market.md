<p><p>InGaAs Avalanche Photodiodes (APDs) are highly sensitive photodetectors that operate in the near-infrared spectrum. They consist of an InGaAs absorption region and an avalanche multiplication region. When photons are absorbed in the absorption region, they generate electron-hole pairs that are accelerated by a high electric field in the avalanche region, leading to a strong internal gain. This gain allows APDs to detect weak optical signals and offers higher sensitivity compared to regular photodiodes.</p><p>The InGaAs Avalanche Photodiodes Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for high-performance photodetectors in various applications such as telecommunications, spectroscopy, military and defense, and industrial processing is the primary driver for market growth.
