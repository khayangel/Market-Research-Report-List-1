<p><p>The chilled food transport market includes two main types: single temperature and multi-temperature. Single temperature refers to the transportation of goods at a constant temperature environment throughout the journey. It involves maintaining a consistent temperature for perishable items such as fruits and vegetables. On the other hand, the multi-temperature market involves transporting different goods requiring varying temperature settings within the same shipment. This type caters to the needs of businesses handling diverse products that demand unique temperature control, such as frozen foods, dairy products, and fresh produce.</p></p>
