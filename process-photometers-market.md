<p><p>Process photometers are optical instruments used to measure the intensity of light beams passing through or reflected from a substance. They are commonly used in various industries for monitoring and controlling processes. There are different types of process photometers, including absorbance photometers, which measure the absorption of light by a substance, turbidity photometers, which measure the scattering of light by suspended particles, and others which encompass a range of specific measurements like fluorescence or colorimetry. These photometers play crucial roles in ensuring product quality and process efficiency in industries such as pharmaceuticals, water treatment, and food and beverage.</p></p>
