<p><p>The embedded display market includes various types of display technologies such as LCD, LED, OLED, and others. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) technology uses liquid crystals to display images and is commonly used in smartphones, televisions, and computer monitors. LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays use tiny LEDs to create images and are energy-efficient and durable, widely used in outdoor advertising and digital signage. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) displays use organic compounds and emit light when an electric current is applied, providing high contrast and flexibility. Other display technologies may include e-paper, plasma, and electroluminescent displays.</p></p>
