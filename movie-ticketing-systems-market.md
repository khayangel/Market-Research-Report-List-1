<p><ul><li>On-Premise</li><li>Cloud-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Movie ticketing systems market can be categorized into two types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise systems are installed and operated within the premises of the movie theater. They require dedicated hardware, software, and technical expertise to manage and maintain the system. On the other hand, Cloud-Based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. They offer more flexibility and scalability as they can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection. Cloud-Based systems also eliminate the need for hardware and software maintenance, making them more cost-effective for movie theaters.</p></p>
