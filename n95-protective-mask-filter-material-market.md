<p><p>The N95 Protective Mask Filter Material market includes two types: PP Nonwoven and PTFE Nonwoven. PP Nonwoven is a type of filter material made from polypropylene fibers. It is widely used in N95 masks due to its excellent filtration efficiency and breathability. On the other hand, PTFE Nonwoven refers to a filter material made from PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) fibers. PTFE Nonwoven offers superior filtration performance, high temperature resistance, and chemical inertness, making it suitable for demanding applications in N95 masks.</p></p>
