These lenses offer enhanced image quality and clarity even in low light and adverse weather conditions, making them suitable for applications like perimeter security, border control, and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs).</p><p>Secondly, the growing use of LWIR lenses in the automotive industry is also contributing to market growth.
