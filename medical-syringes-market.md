<p><p>Medical syringes are devices used to administer medications, inject fluids, or extract bodily fluids, such as blood, from patients. They consist of a barrel, plunger, and a needle, and are available in various sizes and types depending on the specific medical procedure. Medical syringes are commonly used in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare settings.</p><p>The global medical syringes market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 12.60% during the forecast period. Several factors are driving this growth, including an increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, a growing geriatric population, and advancements in technology leading to the development of innovative syringes.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the medical syringes market is the shift towards prefilled syringes. Prefilled syringes are pre-filled with a specific dosage of medication, allowing for easy and accurate administration. They eliminate the need for manual filling, reducing the risk of medication errors and contamination. This trend is gaining traction due to the increasing demand for self-administered injectable medications and the advantages associated with prefilled syringes.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the use of safety syringes, which are designed to prevent needlestick injuries and reduce the risk of infection transmission. Safety syringes have built-in safety mechanisms that either retract or shield the needle after use, mitigating the risk of accidental needlestick injuries among healthcare professionals.</p><p>In conclusion, the medical syringes market is witnessing steady growth with a projected CAGR of 12.60% during the forecast period. The industry is witnessing trends towards the adoption of prefilled syringes and safety syringes, driven by the demand for easy and accurate drug administration and the need for improved safety measures.</p></p>
