<p><p>BOM software, also known as Bill of Materials software, is categorized into three main types: Engineering BOM, Manufacturing BOM, and Service BOM markets. Engineering BOM focuses on creating and managing the list of components and materials required for product design and development. Manufacturing BOM assists in planning and executing the production process by organizing the materials needed per unit. Service BOM regulates the inventory and resources required for servicing and maintaining products. All three market types of BOM software address different aspects of product lifecycle management, streamlining processes from design to production to service.</p></p>
