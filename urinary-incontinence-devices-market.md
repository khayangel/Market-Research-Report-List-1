<p><strong>The Urinary Incontinence Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Urinary Incontinence Devices</li><li>Internal Urinary Incontinence Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Urinary incontinence devices are designed to manage and treat urinary incontinence, a condition characterized by the inability to control urination. There are two main types of these devices: external urinary incontinence devices and internal urinary incontinence devices. </p><p>External urinary incontinence devices, such as absorbent pads or adult diapers, are worn outside the body to absorb urine and prevent leakage. They provide an easy and discreet solution for individuals suffering from mild to moderate urinary incontinence.</p><p>On the other hand, internal urinary incontinence devices are inserted into the body to assist with bladder control. These devices include urethral inserts, vaginal pessaries, or intraurethral valves. They work by either blocking the flow of urine or providing support to the bladder or urethra to prevent leakage.</p><p>Overall, both types of urinary incontinence devices aim to improve the quality of life for individuals experiencing urinary incontinence by effectively managing and addressing the condition.</p></p>
