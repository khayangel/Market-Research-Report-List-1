<p><strong>The Gesture Recognition Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polygonal Imaging</li><li>Light Flying Time</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gesture recognition systems use various technologies for detecting and interpreting human movements. Polygonal imaging involves capturing 2D depth maps and recognizing gestures based on geometric representations. Light flying time relies on measuring the time taken for light to reflect off a surface and return to the sensor to calculate the distance and recognize gestures. Structured light projects a pattern onto the object or user, and the distortion of the pattern is analyzed to determine gestures. These technologies enable gesture recognition systems to accurately interpret and respond to human gestures.</p></p>
