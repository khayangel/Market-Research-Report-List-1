<p><p>CNC Precision Vises are available in two main types - Single Station and Multi-station CNC Precision Vises. Single Station CNC Precision Vises are designed to hold and clamp workpieces in a single position at a time. They are suitable for tasks that require precision and accuracy in machining. On the other hand, Multi-station CNC Precision Vises can hold and clamp multiple workpieces simultaneously. This type is beneficial in high-demand production environments where efficiency and productivity are crucial. Both types cater to different needs and offer specific advantages in the CNC Precision Vises market.</p></p>
