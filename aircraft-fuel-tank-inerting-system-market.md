<p><p>The Aircraft Fuel Tank Inerting System market includes various types such as ASM-based System, Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), and Others. ASM-based systems rely on air separation module technology to remove oxygen from the fuel tank, preventing ignition of fuel vapors. Pressure Swing Adsorption uses adsorbent materials to separate oxygen from the air, ensuring a reduced oxygen concentration in the tank. Lastly, the "Others" category encompasses alternative technologies that also aid in fuel tank inerting, but may have different mechanisms than the two aforementioned types.</p></p>
