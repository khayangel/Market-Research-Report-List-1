<p><strong>The Railway Power Supply Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Power Supply System</li><li>BT Power Supply Mode</li><li>AT Power Supply Mode</li><li>Coaxialcable Power Supply Mode</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway power supply systems refer to the various methods of delivering electricity to power trains. Direct power supply system involves the direct supply of AC or DC power to the overhead lines. BT Power Supply Mode transfers power through a third rail and is commonly used in underground railways. AT Power Supply Mode employs an overhead catenary system to provide power. Coaxial cable power supply mode utilizes a coaxial cable system for power transmission. Other markets include new technologies emerging in the railway power supply sector, such as energy storage systems and renewable energy integration.</p></p>
