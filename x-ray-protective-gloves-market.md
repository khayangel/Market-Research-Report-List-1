<p><p>Lead gloves and lead-free gloves are both types of X-ray protective gloves used in medical imaging to protect healthcare workers and patients from harmful radiation exposure. Lead gloves contain lead lining that effectively blocks radiation, providing high levels of protection. On the other hand, lead-free gloves are made with alternative materials that are just as effective in blocking radiation but do not contain any lead, making them a safer and more environmentally friendly option. Both types of gloves are essential for ensuring safety in radiology departments and other medical settings.</p></p>
