<p><strong>The Dry Film Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Coating Thickness Gauges</li><li>Mechanical Coating Thickness Gauges</li><li>Destructive Coating Thickness Gauges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry film gauge market encompasses three main types: digital coating thickness gauges, mechanical coating thickness gauges, and destructive coating thickness gauges. Digital coating thickness gauges rely on electronic technology to measure the thickness of dry films. Mechanical coating thickness gauges use mechanical principles and a spring-loaded mechanism to determine thickness. Destructive coating thickness gauges involve removing a sample from the coating and measuring its thickness using a microscope or other measurement devices. Each type offers different advantages and is suitable for specific applications within the coating industry.</p></p>
