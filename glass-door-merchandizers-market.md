<p><p>Glass door merchandizers are available in two main types: hinged door type and sliding door type. Hinged door merchandizers have doors on one side that swing open, providing easy access to the products. These are commonly used in smaller spaces where space is limited. On the other hand, sliding door merchandizers have doors that slide horizontally, allowing for a wider opening and better visibility of the products. This type is frequently used in larger spaces where a more convenient and attractive display is desired.</p></p>
