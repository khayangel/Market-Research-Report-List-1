<p><p>Computer Reservation Systems (CRS) are software applications used in the travel and hospitality industry to manage and distribute travel-related services. There are two main market types for CRS: on-premise and web-based. On-premise CRS are installed and operated on local servers, giving companies full control but requiring high upfront costs. In contrast, web-based CRS operate on remote servers accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. Both types enable travel businesses to automate processes, manage reservations, and share inventory with various distribution channels.</p></p>
