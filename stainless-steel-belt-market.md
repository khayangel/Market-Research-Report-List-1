<p><p>The stainless steel belt market includes two main types: straight line and curve belts. Straight line belts are designed for applications that require linear movement, such as conveying products or materials in a straight line without any changes in direction. On the other hand, curve belts are used when the application requires the belt to move in a curved path, allowing for more flexibility and adaptability in machinery and production processes. Both types serve different purposes and are essential for various industries.</p></p>
