<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Special Purpose Logic IC Market consists of various types of IC designs: Full-custom Design IC, Standard-cell Based IC, and Gate-array Based IC. </p><p>Full-custom Design IC refers to designing and manufacturing ICs from scratch, providing high performance and customization but requiring significant time and cost. </p><p>Standard-cell Based ICs utilize pre-designed standard cells, offering a balance between customization and affordability by allowing designers to select and connect these cells. </p><p>Gate-array Based ICs employ pre-designed logic gates that can be customized by configuring internal interconnections. These ICs offer flexibility, reduced design time, and lower costs compared to full-custom design.</p></p>
