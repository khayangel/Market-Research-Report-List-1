<p><p>Double check valves, also known as double check valve assemblies (DCVAs), are mechanical devices used to prevent the backflow of water or other fluids in plumbing systems. They consist of two independently operated check valves that are placed in series, providing an extra layer of protection in situations where one check valve may fail.</p><p>The future outlook for the double check valves market appears positive. The increasing adoption of DCVAs in various industries, including residential, commercial, and industrial sectors, is expected to drive market growth.
