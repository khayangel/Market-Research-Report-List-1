<p><p>The biosafety cabinets for dispensing market consists of three main types: Class II A Type, Class II B Type, and Class III Type. Class II A Type cabinets provide personnel protection, suitable for low-risk biological agents. Class II B Type cabinets provide both personnel and product protection, suitable for moderate-risk biological agents. Class III Type cabinets are totally enclosed and gas-tight, providing maximum protection for handling high-risk biological agents. These cabinets are designed to ensure the safety of personnel, the environment, and the product being dispensed.</p></p>
