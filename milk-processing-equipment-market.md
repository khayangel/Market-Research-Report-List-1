Homogenization equipment helps break down the fat molecules in milk, preventing cream separation. Separation equipment is used to separate milk into its different components, such as cream and skim milk. Filtration equipment is utilized to remove impurities and particles from milk. Lastly, the "other" category encompasses various equipment used in milk processing, such as pasteurization machines and packaging equipment.</p></p>
