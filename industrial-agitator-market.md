<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The industrial agitator market includes three main types of agitators - top-entry, side-entry, and bottom-entry agitators. Top-entry agitators are installed from the top of the tank and are suitable for large tanks. Side-entry agitators are installed on the side of the tank and offer efficient mixing for medium to large tanks. Bottom-entry agitators are installed from the bottom and are commonly used in small tanks. These different types of agitators cater to various tank sizes and provide effective mixing solutions for industrial processes.</p></p>
