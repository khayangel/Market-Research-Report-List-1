<p><p>Programmable Logic Control Systems (PLC) are used in industrial automation for controlling and monitoring manufacturing processes. Compact PLCs are small, stand-alone controllers that are ideal for simple applications with limited input/output (I/O) needs. They are cost-effective and easy to install. On the other hand, modular PLCs consist of separate units or modules that can be customized and expanded to meet complex automation requirements. They offer greater flexibility and scalability, allowing for more inputs and outputs, communication options, and programming capabilities. Both types play a crucial role in achieving automation and productivity in various industries.</p></p>
