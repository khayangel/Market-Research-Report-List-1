<p><p>The Voltage Signal Amplifier Market includes two major types: Digital and Analog amplifiers. Digital amplifiers use binary code to process the input signal, converting it into a series of on/off pulses. This type offers high accuracy, efficiency, and flexibility, making it suitable for complex applications. In contrast, Analog amplifiers process the input signal in its original waveform, providing a continuous output that faithfully replicates the input. These amplifiers are widely used due to their simplicity, low cost, and compatibility with a wide range of applications.</p></p>
