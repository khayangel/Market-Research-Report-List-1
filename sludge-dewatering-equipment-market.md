<p><p>Sludge dewatering equipment market consists of various types of equipment used for removing water from sludge, facilitating its disposal or further treatment. The most common types are screw press, rotator disc press, centrifuges, belt filter press, and other niche equipment. Screw presses utilize a rotating screw to squeeze water out of sludge. Rotator disc presses use rotating discs for the same purpose. Centrifuges separate water from sludge by rotating it at high speeds. Belt filter presses employ a series of belts to extract water. Other niche equipment may include vacuum filters or decanter centrifuges.</p></p>
