<p><p>Adult knee sleeves come in two main types: elastic and non-elastic knee sleeves. Elastic knee sleeves are made of stretchy materials like neoprene that provide compression and support to the knee joint. They are commonly used during physical activities to reduce pain and swelling. Non-elastic knee sleeves, on the other hand, are usually made of fabric materials without much stretch. They offer mild support and comfort for daily activities but may not provide as much compression as elastic knee sleeves.</p></p>
