<p><p>The lifting and traversing class is a type of three-dimensional parking lot where vehicles are lifted and moved horizontally to maximize space. Simple lift parking lots involve a vertical lift system that moves cars to different levels. Roadway stacking involves a multi-level parking facility where cars are parked in a stacked manner on a horizontal roadway. Plane movement parking lots use a horizontal movement system to park vehicles on different levels. Vertical lift parking lots lift cars from one vertical level to another. Vertical loop parking lots use a loop system to move cars vertically and horizontally for parking.</p></p>
