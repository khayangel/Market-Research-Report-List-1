<p><p>DNA Purification Market Types refer to various methods used to isolate and obtain pure DNA samples for research and diagnostic purposes. Alcohol Purification involves precipitation of DNA using alcohol, while Silica Gel Column Based Purification employs columns filled with silica beads to bind and separate DNA from other cellular components. Agarose Gel-based DNA Purification involves using agarose gel electrophoresis to separate DNA fragments of different sizes. Automated DNA Purification utilizes automated systems for efficient and high-throughput DNA isolation. Chemical Based DNA Purification uses chemicals and enzymes to extract and purify DNA from samples. These methods contribute to the growing DNA purification market.</p></p>
