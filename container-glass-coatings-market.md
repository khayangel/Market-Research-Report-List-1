<p><p>Container glass coatings market can be divided into two types: water-based and oil-based coatings. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, easily applicable, and offer good adhesion to glass surfaces. They are preferred for their low VOC content and fast drying times. On the other hand, oil-based coatings provide excellent protection against scratches and abrasions, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Both types of coatings have their own advantages and are commonly used in the container glass industry.</p></p>
