<p><p>The smart surge protector market comprises three main types of products: power strips, sockets, and others. Power strips are multiple-outlet devices that provide surge protection for a variety of electronic devices. Sockets, on the other hand, are single-outlet surge protectors that are directly plugged into wall outlets. The "others" category encompasses innovative surge protector designs like wall-mounted units or ones integrated into power cords. These market types aim to provide consumers with a range of options to safeguard their electronics against power surges, while offering convenience and flexibility in terms of usage.</p></p>
