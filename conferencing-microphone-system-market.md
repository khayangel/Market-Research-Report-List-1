<p><p>Conferencing microphone systems are available in two main types: wired and wireless. On the other hand, wireless systems operate without cables, using advanced technologies like Bluetooth or Wi-Fi to transmit audio signals. Wireless systems provide greater mobility and flexibility as users can move around freely during conferences. Both types offer different advantages and are suitable for various conferencing environments based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
