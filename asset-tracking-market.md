<p><p>Asset tracking is the process of keeping track of an organization's physical assets, including equipment, vehicles, machinery, tools, and other resources. It involves using various technologies like RFID (Radio Frequency Identification), GPS (Global Positioning System), and barcode scanners to monitor and locate these assets in real-time. Asset tracking helps companies optimize their asset utilization, streamline operations, prevent theft or loss, improve maintenance planning, and enhance overall efficiency.</p><p>The asset tracking market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory.
