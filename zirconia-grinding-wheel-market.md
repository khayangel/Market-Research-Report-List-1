<p><ul><li>Flat</li><li>Conical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zirconia grinding wheel market can be categorized into two types: flat and conical wheels. Flat wheels are characterized by their flat and smooth surface, making them suitable for grinding surfaces with greater contact area and providing a more consistent finish. On the other hand, conical wheels have a tapered shape, enabling them to reach tight corners and angles that may be difficult to access with flat wheels. Consequently, conical wheels are commonly used for precision grinding and polishing tasks that require maneuverability and intricate detail work.</p></p>
