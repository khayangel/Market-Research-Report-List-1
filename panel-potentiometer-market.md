<p><p>There are various types of Panel Potentiometer market available. PC Pin potentiometers are typically mounted directly onto a printed circuit board with the help of pins. Through Hole potentiometers are also mounted on circuit boards but with larger pins that pass through holes on the board for added strength. Wire Lead potentiometers have long wire leads instead of pins, making them ideal for applications where the potentiometer needs to be located away from the circuit board. Lastly, the "Other" market may include specialized potentiometers designed for specific applications or those that do not fit into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
