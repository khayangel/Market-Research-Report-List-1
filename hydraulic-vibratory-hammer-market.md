<p><p>The hydraulic vibratory hammer market offers two types: conventional and high frequency. The conventional type operates with a lower frequency and is suited for applications such as sheet pile driving and soil compacting. On the other hand, the high frequency type operates with a higher frequency and is ideal for driving steel tubes and concrete piles. Both types use hydraulic power to generate vibrations, but they are designed to cater to different needs in construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
