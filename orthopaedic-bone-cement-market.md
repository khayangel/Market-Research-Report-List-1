<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Cements</li><li>Medium Viscosity Cements</li><li>High Viscosity Cements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopaedic bone cement is used in joint replacement surgeries to fill the gap between the orthopedic implant and the existing bone, providing stability and facilitating the healing process. Low viscosity cements have a lower flowability and are suitable for vertebroplasty procedures. Medium viscosity cements have moderate flowability and are commonly used in hip and knee replacements. High viscosity cements have the highest flowability and are used in complex fractures and revision surgeries. The choice of cement viscosity depends on the specific surgical procedure and bone condition.</p></p>
