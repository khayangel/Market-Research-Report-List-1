<p><p>The commercial misting system market consists of three types: low-pressure, medium-pressure, and high-pressure systems. Low-pressure misting systems generate a fine mist by using fans and pumps, often used in outdoor settings such as patios or gardens. Medium-pressure systems employ a combination of fans, pumps, and nozzles to create smaller water droplets, offering a more efficient and effective cooling solution for restaurants or warehouses. High-pressure misting systems utilize high-pressure pumps to produce tiny water particles, ideal for cooling large areas in stadiums, theme parks, or outdoor events.</p></p>
