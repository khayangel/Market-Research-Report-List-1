<p><strong>The Mobile Camera Module VCM Driver IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Loop VCM Driver IC</li><li>Closed-Loop VCM Driver IC</li><li>Optical Anti-Shake (OIS) VCM Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile Camera Module VCM Driver IC market includes three types: Open-Loop VCM Driver IC, Closed-Loop VCM Driver IC, and Optical Anti-Shake (OIS) VCM Driver IC. Open-Loop VCM Driver IC technology is used to drive Voice Coil Motors (VCM) without feedback, resulting in cost-effective solutions for low-end smartphones. Closed-Loop VCM Driver IC uses feedback control to enhance performance accuracy, catering to mid-range and high-end smartphones. Optical Anti-Shake (OIS) VCM Driver IC addresses image stabilization requirements, reducing blur caused by camera movement and enabling crisp, stable images on high-end smartphones.</p></p>
