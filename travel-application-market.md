<p><p>The travel application market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based travel applications are hosted on remote servers and users can access them through the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as they can be easily accessed from any device with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premise travel applications are installed and maintained on the user's local infrastructure. They provide more control and customization options, but require higher upfront costs and dedicated IT resources for maintenance and upgrades.</p></p>
