<p><p>The Aerospace Elastomers market consists of three main types: Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM), Silicone Elastomers, and Fluoroelastomers. EPDM elastomers are known for their excellent resistance to weathering, ozone, and heat, making them suitable for exterior applications in aerospace. Silicone elastomers offer high temperature resistance and electrical insulation properties, making them useful in aerospace electrical systems. Fluoroelastomers, on the other hand, provide exceptional resistance to fuels, oils, and chemicals, making them ideal for demanding aerospace applications such as seals and gaskets. These elastomers play a vital role in ensuring safety and performance in aerospace engineering.</p></p>
