<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Miniature injection molding machines are categorized into three types: hydraulic, all-electric, and hybrid. </p><p>Hydraulic machines use hydraulic power to apply pressure during the injection process. They are typically cost-effective and suitable for high-volume production. </p><p>All-electric machines utilize electric power for all machine movements, resulting in precise control and energy efficiency. They are ideal for cleanroom environments and applications requiring high-precision molding.</p><p>Hybrid machines combine both hydraulic and electric systems, offering the benefits of both types. They are suitable for a wide range of applications, providing improved energy efficiency and high performance.</p></p>
