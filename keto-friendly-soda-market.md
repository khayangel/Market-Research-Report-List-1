<p><p>Keto-friendly soda refers to carbonated beverages that are suitable for individuals following the ketogenic diet. Traditional sodas typically contain high levels of sugar or artificial sweeteners, which are not compatible with the principles of the keto diet. To cater to the growing demand from keto dieters, several companies have introduced keto-friendly sodas that are free from high-carb ingredients and are sweetened with natural alternatives such as stevia or erythritol.</p><p>The Keto-Friendly Soda Market is experiencing steady growth due to a surge in the popularity of the ketogenic diet and the increasing consumer preference for healthier beverage options.
