<p><ul><li>Flat Type</li><li>Slot Type</li><li>Oblique Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable dental articulators are devices used in dentistry to hold dental models in a stable position during procedures. There are three main types of disposable dental articulators: flat type, slot type, and oblique type. </p><p>The flat type articulator has a flat surface on which the dental models are placed. This type provides a stable and fixed position for the models.</p><p>The slot type articulator has slots on which the dental models are secured. These slots allow for adjustments and flexibility in positioning the models.</p><p>The oblique type articulator has an inclined plane on which the dental models are positioned. This type allows for angulation adjustments and better simulation of natural jaw movement during procedures.</p></p>
