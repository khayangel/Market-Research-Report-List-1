<p><p>The floating house market includes three main types: floating homes and cottages, houseboats, and amphibious houses. Floating homes and cottages are built on floating platforms and are permanently moored to a shoreline. Houseboats, on the other hand, are designed to be mobile and can be used for leisure or as primary residences. Amphibious houses are structures that are designed to float during floods and rest on the ground during dry periods. These market segments cater to different lifestyles and needs, offering unique experiences of living on the water.</p></p>
