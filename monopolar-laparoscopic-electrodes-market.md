<p><p>The monopolar laparoscopic electrodes market is categorized into two types: disposable and reusable electrodes. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use and are discarded after each surgical procedure. They offer convenience, reduced risk of cross-contamination, and ease of use. Reusable electrodes, on the other hand, can be sterilized and used multiple times. They are more cost-effective in the long run but require proper cleaning and maintenance. Both types cater to the needs of laparoscopic surgeries, but their usage differs based on preferences, infection control protocols, and cost considerations.</p></p>
