<p><strong>The Floating Storage and Offloading Unit (FSO) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Floating Storage and Offloading (FSO)</li><li>Floating Production Storage and Offloading (FPSO)</li><li>Floating Drilling Production Storage and Offloading (FDPSO)</li><li>Floating Storage Regasification Unit (FSRU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floating Storage and Offloading (FSO) refers to a unit that stores and transfers oil and gas in offshore operations. Floating Production Storage and Offloading (FPSO) units are similar but also have the capability to produce hydrocarbons. Floating Drilling Production Storage and Offloading (FDPSO) units combine drilling, production, and storage functionalities. Lastly, the Floating Storage Regasification Unit (FSRU) is used to receive, store, and regasify liquefied natural gas before it is transported through pipelines. These units are crucial in the offshore oil and gas industry for storage, production, and transportation purposes.</p></p>
