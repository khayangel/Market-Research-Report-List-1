<p><p>A digital business card is a virtual representation of a traditional business card, designed to be easily shared and accessible through digital platforms. With digital cards, individuals can easily share their contact information via email, text message, QR codes, or through various social media platforms. This convenience, combined with the ability to capture and store multiple digital cards on a single device, has made digital business cards a popular choice among professionals.</p><p>Moreover, digital business cards are environmentally friendly, eliminating the need for printing physical cards and reducing paper waste.
