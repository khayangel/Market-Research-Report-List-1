<p><ul><li>Carbohydrase</li><li>Protease</li><li>Lipase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Baking enzymes market is segmented into three major types: carbohydrase, protease, and lipase. Carbohydrases are enzymes used to break down complex carbohydrates into simpler forms, aiding in dough development and improving bread volume and texture. Proteases are responsible for protein breakdown, enhancing dough handling and improving gluten structure. Lipases catalyze the breakdown of lipids or fats, leading to improved dough stability and bread softness. These enzymes play a crucial role in the baking industry by improving product quality, extending shelf life, and optimizing the production process.</p></p>
