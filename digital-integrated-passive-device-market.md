<p><p>The digital integrated passive device (IPD) market can be categorized into two types: Silicon Digital Integrated Passive Device and Non-Silicon Digital Integrated Passive Device. Silicon IPDs are fabricated using silicon-based technologies and offer advantages such as high performance, miniaturization, and compatibility with semiconductor manufacturing processes.
