<p><p>Bronze Sintered Powder Filters are used for filtration purposes in various industries. These filters are categorized into three types based on their porosity levels. Low Porosity filters have a porosity level below 30%, making them suitable for applications requiring fine filtration. Medium Porosity filters, with a porosity level ranging from 30% to 60%, strike a balance between flow rate and filtration efficiency. High Porosity filters have a porosity level above 60% and offer high flow rates at the expense of filtration efficiency. These different filter types cater to diverse filtration needs across industries.</p></p>
