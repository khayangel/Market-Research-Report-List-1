<p><p>The parking barrier gate market is divided into two types: straight and crank gates. Straight barrier gates are usually made of a single straight bar that lifts vertically to allow or block access. They are commonly used in parking lots and toll booths. On the other hand, crank barrier gates consist of a vertical bar that is attached to a crank mechanism. This mechanism allows the gate to be lifted and locked at different heights, providing flexibility in controlling vehicle access in different situations, such as at security checkpoints or industrial sites.</p></p>
