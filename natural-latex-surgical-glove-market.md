<p><p>Natural latex surgical gloves are medical gloves made from natural rubber latex. One of the key drivers of market growth is the increasing number of surgical procedures being performed worldwide. The rising prevalence of chronic diseases, coupled with the growing geriatric population, has led to a surge in the demand for surgical procedures, which, in turn, has boosted the demand for natural latex surgical gloves.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing awareness about the importance of maintaining hygiene and preventing the spread of infections has also contributed to market growth. Stringent regulations and guidelines regarding healthcare-associated infections have led to the widespread adoption of natural latex surgical gloves in healthcare settings, further driving market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, there has been a shift towards the use of powder-free gloves in the natural latex surgical glove market. Powder-free gloves offer numerous benefits such as reduced risk of latex allergies, improved grip, and enhanced tactile sensitivity.
