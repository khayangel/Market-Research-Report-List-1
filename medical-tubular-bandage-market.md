<p><p>The medical tubular bandage market consists of two main types: tubular elastic support bandage and tubular elastic compression bandage. Tubular elastic support bandages provide gentle support and pressure for sprains, strains, and general muscle weakness. They help stabilize joints and prevent further injury. On the other hand, tubular elastic compression bandages exert stronger pressure to improve circulation and reduce swelling in conditions like lymphedema or venous ulcers. Both types are versatile, easy to apply, and commonly used in medical settings for various purposes.</p></p>
