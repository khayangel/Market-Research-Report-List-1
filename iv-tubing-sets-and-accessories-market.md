<p><p>IV tubing sets and accessories are essential medical devices used for the administration of fluids, medications, and other treatments directly into the patient's bloodstream. Different types of IV tubing sets and accessories cater to specific procedures. Peripheral intravenous catheter insertion involves inserting a small tube into a vein on the patient's arm. Central venous catheter placement is the insertion of a catheter into a large vein in the patient's neck, chest, or groin. PICC line insertion involves inserting a long catheter into a vein near the patient's arm to deliver treatments over an extended period.</p></p>
