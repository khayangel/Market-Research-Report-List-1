<p><p>Food absorbent pads are used in packaging to absorb excess liquid from fresh food products and maintain their quality and shelf life. There are two main types of food absorbent pads: hard pads and soft pads. Hard pads are rigid and provide a firm support to the packaged food, preventing any leakage or contamination. On the other hand, soft pads are more flexible and conform to the shape of the food, providing cushioning and absorbing liquids without causing any damage. Both types are crucial in ensuring proper packaging and preserving the freshness of food products.</p></p>
