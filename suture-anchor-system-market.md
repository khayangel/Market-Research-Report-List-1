<p><p>A suture anchor system is a medical device used in orthopedic surgeries to anchor soft tissues to bones. It consists of a suture anchor, which is implanted into the bone, and a suture, which is used to secure the soft tissue to the anchor. This system provides stability and support to damaged or torn tissues, allowing for proper healing.</p><p>The global suture anchor system market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period. These anchors dissolve over time, eliminating the need for a second surgery to remove them. Biodegradable anchors reduce the risk of infection, reduce post-operative pain, and improve patient outcomes.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing use of suture anchor systems in minimally invasive surgeries.
