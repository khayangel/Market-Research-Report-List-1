<p><p>The single sided flexible printed circuit market mainly comprises two types: aluminum-based and copper-based. Aluminum-based circuits are made using an aluminum substrate, offering lightweight and cost-effective solutions. They are suitable for low-frequency applications and can withstand high temperatures. On the other hand, copper-based circuits use a copper substrate, providing better electrical conductivity and flexibility. They are commonly used in high-frequency applications and are more durable. Both types have their pros and cons, making them suitable for different customer requirements in the flexible printed circuit market.</p></p>
