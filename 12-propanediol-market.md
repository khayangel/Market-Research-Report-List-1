The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for 1,2-Propanediol in various end-use applications, such as polyester resins, antifreeze and coolants, detergents, paints and coatings, and lubricants. Additionally, the growing demand for 1,2-Propanediol as a substitute for ethylene glycol, which is known to have toxic properties, is also contributing to the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising awareness about the environmental and health hazards associated with petrochemical-based products has led to a shift towards the use of bio-based 1,2-Propanediol.
