<p><p>A Light Duty Rollator is a type of mobility aid that is designed to assist individuals with limited strength or mobility in walking and standing. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing geriatric population worldwide. With the aging population, there is a growing demand for mobility aids to improve the independence and quality of life for elderly individuals.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the production of innovative lightweight rollators that offer enhanced maneuverability and comfort. Manufacturers are investing in research and development to introduce features such as adjustable height, collapsible frames, and improved braking systems, further driving market growth.</p><p>The rising prevalence of chronic diseases and disabilities also fuels the demand for light duty rollators. These mobility aids provide support and stability, making it easier for individuals with conditions such as arthritis, osteoporosis, and multiple sclerosis to move around and perform daily activities.</p><p>Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the importance of personal mobility and independence.
