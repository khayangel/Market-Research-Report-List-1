<p><p>The Ultra High Purity Buffered Hydrofluoric Acid (BHF) market consists of four main types: UP/SEMI G4, UP-S/SEMI G3, UP-SS/SEMI G2, and EL/SEMI G1. UP/SEMI G4 BHF offers the highest level of purity, suitable for advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes. UP-S/SEMI G3 BHF has a slightly lower purity level but is still ideal for most semiconductor fabrication applications. UP-SS/SEMI G2 BHF contains even lesser impurities and is commonly used in etching semiconductor wafers. EL/SEMI G1 BHF has the lowest purity level and is typically employed in less demanding applications, such as cleaning or etching glass.</p></p>
