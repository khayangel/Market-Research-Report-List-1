<p><strong>The Hot Water Buffer Tank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Buffer Tank</li><li>Air Bag Buffer Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot water buffer tanks are used in heating systems to store and regulate hot water supply. Diaphragm buffer tanks feature a flexible diaphragm that separates the water from an air chamber, preventing waterlogging and maintaining system pressure. This type ensures consistent water flow and reduces energy consumption. Meanwhile, air bag buffer tanks utilize a replaceable air bag that expands and contracts to accommodate water volume changes. This design reduces system wear and tear, increasing its lifespan. Both tank types enhance heating system efficiency and performance, providing a reliable supply of hot water.</p></p>
