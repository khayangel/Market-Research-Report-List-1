This has gained popularity in the gaming and entertainment industry as it provides a more convenient and immersive experience for users. Another trend is the integration of 3D display modules in smartphones and tablets, enabling users to view 3D content without the need for separate devices.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a growing demand for holographic displays, which create virtual 3D objects in space.
