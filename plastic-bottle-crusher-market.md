<p><p>The market for Plastic Bottle Crushers can be classified into two types: Single-axis and Multi-axis. Single-axis Plastic Bottle Crushers have a single rotating shaft that crushes the bottles by applying pressure from one side. They are generally smaller in size and ideal for domestic use. On the other hand, Multi-axis Plastic Bottle Crushers have multiple rotating shafts that crush the bottles from multiple angles, resulting in efficient crushing of larger quantities. These crushers are typically used in commercial settings such as restaurants, hotels, and recycling facilities.</p></p>
