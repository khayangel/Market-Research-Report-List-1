<p><p>Animation design software is comprised of two main market types: 2D animation and 3D animation. 2D animation refers to creating movement in a two-dimensional space, using techniques like drawing, hand-drawing, or vector graphics. This type is commonly used for simpler animations and traditional cartoon-like effects. On the other hand, 3D animation involves creating movement in a three-dimensional space, utilizing advanced modeling, rigging, and texturing techniques. It provides more realistic and immersive animations, often employed in movies, video games, and virtual reality experiences. These market types cater to different animation needs and serve diverse industries.</p></p>
