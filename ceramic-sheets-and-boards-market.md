<p><p>Ceramic sheets and boards market includes various types such as wear-resistant ceramic chips, piezoelectric ceramic pieces, and thermal conductive ceramic chips. Wear-resistant ceramic chips are used to protect surfaces from abrasion and erosion. Piezoelectric ceramic pieces convert mechanical stress into electrical signals, commonly used in sensors and transducers. Thermal conductive ceramic chips are designed to efficiently transfer heat, suitable for applications requiring thermal management. Each type serves a specific purpose in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
