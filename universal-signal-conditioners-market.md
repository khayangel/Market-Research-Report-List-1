<p><p>Universal signal conditioners are electronic devices that convert and amplify signals from sensors or transducers to compatible output signals for various measurement instruments or control systems. They are available in different types based on the number of input channels. </p><p>Single-channel universal signal conditioners can handle one input signal and provide a corresponding output signal. Dual-channel signal conditioners have two independent input channels and can process two signals simultaneously. Multi-channel signal conditioners are designed to handle multiple input signals, typically four or more channels, offering the ability to process and transmit signals from multiple sensors or transducers simultaneously.</p></p>
