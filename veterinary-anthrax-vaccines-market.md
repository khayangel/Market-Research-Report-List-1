<p><p>The veterinary anthrax vaccines market includes two types: Cell Free PA Vaccines and Live Vaccines. Cell Free PA Vaccines are developed by extracting a protective antigen (PA) from Bacillus anthracis bacteria and inactivating it. These vaccines provide protection against anthrax without the risk of causing the actual disease. On the other hand, Live Vaccines contain weakened or attenuated strains of the bacteria that can stimulate an immune response without causing severe illness. Both types of vaccines are crucial in preventing anthrax infections in animals.</p></p>
