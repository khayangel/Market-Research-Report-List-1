<p><ul><li>Chromosome Enumeration Probes</li><li>Microdeletion Probes</li><li>Preimplantation Probes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hematology probes are used in various diagnostic tests to detect and analyze chromosomal abnormalities. Chromosome enumeration probes are used to identify the number of specific chromosomes in a cell, while microdeletion probes target small genetic deletions associated with certain disorders. Preimplantation probes are used in genetic testing of embryos before implantation in assisted reproductive technologies. These probes play a crucial role in identifying genetic abnormalities and guiding treatment decisions in hematology and reproductive medicine.</p></p>
