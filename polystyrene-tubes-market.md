<p><ul><li>Round Bottom</li><li>Conical Bottom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polystyrene tubes are available in two main types: round bottom and conical bottom. Round bottom tubes have a circular base and are commonly used for general laboratory applications, including sample storage and centrifugation. Conical bottom tubes, on the other hand, have a tapered bottom to facilitate the separation of liquid and solid components during centrifugation. They are particularly useful for applications that require greater sample recovery, such as DNA extraction and protein precipitation. Both types of tubes are widely used in the scientific and medical research fields.</p></p>
