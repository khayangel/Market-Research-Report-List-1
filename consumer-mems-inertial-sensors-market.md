<p><p>Consumer MEMS inertial sensors market consists of three main types: accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers. Accelerometers are widely used to measure acceleration, tilt, and vibration in devices such as smartphones and gaming controllers. Gyroscopes, on the other hand, measure rotational motion and angular velocity, enabling features like gesture recognition and image stabilization in consumer electronics. Magnetometers are utilized to detect magnetic fields, aiding in applications like compass functionality in smartphones and tablets. These sensors cater to the increasing demand for motion sensing and positioning capabilities in various consumer devices.</p></p>
