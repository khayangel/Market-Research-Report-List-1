<p><p>Industrial Wireless Sensor Network (IWSN) is a network of sensors that communicate with each other wirelessly to monitor and control various industrial processes. Firstly, the increasing demand for automation and process control in industries is driving the adoption of wireless sensor networks. IWSN allows industries to deploy sensors in remote and hard-to-reach locations, providing better monitoring and control capabilities. Additionally, the advantage of reduced installation costs and flexibility in expanding the network is further driving the market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the integration of IoT (Internet of Things) technology in industrial applications is propelling the demand for IWSN. The ability of wireless sensors to gather and transmit real-time data to connected devices enhances operational efficiency and decision-making capabilities. This has led to increased adoption of IWSN in various industries such as manufacturing, oil and gas, power, and chemical.</p><p>Moreover, the advancements in wireless communication technologies, such as Wi-Fi, ZigBee, and Bluetooth, have improved the reliability and range of IWSN.
