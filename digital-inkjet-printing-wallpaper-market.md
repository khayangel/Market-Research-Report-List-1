<p><p>Digital inkjet printing wallpaper market can be categorized into three types - nonwoven, vinyl, and paper. Nonwoven wallpaper is made from synthetic fibers, offering durability, easy installation, and moisture resistance. Vinyl wallpaper is made from polyvinyl chloride, known for its waterproof and washable properties, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. Paper wallpaper is made from cellulose fibers, providing a more traditional and environmentally friendly option. Each type has its unique features and benefits, catering to different customer preferences and needs in the wallpaper market.</p></p>
