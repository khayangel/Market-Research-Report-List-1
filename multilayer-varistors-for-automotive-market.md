<p><p>Multilayer Varistors are electronic components used in the automotive industry as protection against voltage surges and transient disturbances. There are two types available in the market. Surface-mount device (SMD) Multilayer Varistors are small and lightweight, making them suitable for compact automotive designs. Through-hole Multilayer Varistors, on the other hand, have leads that pass through the circuit board, providing reliable contact and greater flexibility in automotive applications. Both types are essential for safeguarding automotive electronics from potential damage caused by voltage fluctuations.</p></p>
