<p><ul><li>IGBT-IPM</li><li>MOSFET-IPM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) are electronic devices used for controlling and driving electric motors. They integrate various power components such as insulated gate bipolar transistors (IGBTs) or metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors (MOSFETs) along with gate drivers, protection circuits, and control logic onto a single module. IGBT-IPMs utilize IGBTs for high-power applications, offering efficient switching and lower conduction losses. On the other hand, MOSFET-IPMs use MOSFETs for low-power applications, providing better heat dissipation and faster switching speeds. Both types cater to different power requirements and exhibit unique characteristics essential for motor control and power management.</p></p>
