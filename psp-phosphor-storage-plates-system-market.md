<p><p>The PSP (Phosphor Storage Plates) System is a medical imaging technology that is widely used in dental radiography. It utilizes a reusable imaging plate coated with a photostimulable phosphor material that absorbs X-ray radiation. The X-rays are converted into light energy, which is stored in the phosphor layer, and then released as visible light when stimulated by a laser, allowing the creation of a digital image.</p><p>The PSP System has gained popularity in recent years due to its advantages over traditional X-ray films, such as higher image resolution, lower radiation dose, and the ability to enhance or manipulate the image digitally. These factors have contributed to the growing adoption of PSP systems in dental clinics and hospitals.</p><p>According to market research, the PSP System Market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years.
