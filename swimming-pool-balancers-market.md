<p><p>Swimming pool balancers can be classified into two main market types: solid and liquid. Solid balancers are typically in a powder or tablet form that can be added directly to the pool water. They often contain chemicals such as calcium carbonate or sodium bicarbonate to increase alkalinity or raise calcium levels. On the other hand, liquid balancers are in a liquid form and are usually added directly to the water using a dosing pump or by pouring. They generally consist of acidic or basic solutions to adjust pH levels or reduce alkalinity in the pool water.</p></p>
