<p><p>The digital dental materials market can be categorized into two types: chairside digital dental materials and laboratory digital dental materials. Chairside digital dental materials are used directly in the dental office, allowing for same-day treatments and eliminating the need to send impressions to a laboratory. On the other hand, laboratory digital dental materials are used in dental laboratories to fabricate various dental prosthetics, including crowns, bridges, dentures, and orthodontic appliances. Both types of materials play a crucial role in modern dentistry, providing efficient and accurate solutions.</p></p>
