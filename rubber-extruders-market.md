<p><p>Rubber extruders are machines used to shape and form rubber compounds into various products. There are two main types: cold feed rubber extruders and hot feed rubber extruders. Cold feed rubber extruders are designed to process uncured rubber materials at lower temperatures, while hot feed rubber extruders use preheated rubber compounds for processing. Cold feed extruders are preferred for more heat-sensitive rubber compounds, while hot feed extruders offer better control and efficiency for high-volume production of rubber products. Both types are vital in the rubber industry for manufacturing various rubber-based products.</p></p>
