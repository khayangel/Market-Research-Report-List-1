<p><p>The BGA rework station market offers two main types of tools: hot air and infrared (IR). Hot air rework stations use controlled temperatures of heated air to melt and remove BGA components for repair or replacement. IR rework stations, on the other hand, utilize infrared technology to directly heat the board, efficiently reflowing or removing solder joints without affecting the surrounding components. Both types provide effective solutions for BGA rework, each with their own advantages and applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
