<p><p>Technical coated textiles come in various types, including PU-coated, rubber-coated, silicone-coated, PVC-coated, and other coatings. PU-coated textiles are durable and water-resistant, rubber-coated fabrics are known for their flexibility and elasticity, silicone-coated materials offer high temperature resistance, and PVC-coated textiles provide excellent chemical and abrasion resistance. Other coatings may include acrylic, fluoropolymer, or wax coatings, each offering specific benefits such as UV protection, flame retardancy, or anti-microbial properties. The diverse range of coatings allows for customization based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
