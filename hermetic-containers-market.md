<p><ul><li>Passivation Glass</li><li>Ceramic-Metal</li><li>Reed-type Glass</li><li>Glass-Metal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hermetic Containers Market can be categorized into various types. Passivation Glass refers to containers made of glass that are treated with a passivation process to enhance their resistance to corrosion. Ceramic-Metal containers are made by combining ceramic and metal materials, providing a high level of protection against moisture and other harmful elements. Reed-type Glass containers feature a reed switch encapsulated in glass, ensuring hermetic sealing and electrical connection. Lastly, Glass-Metal containers are constructed using a combination of glass and metal, providing a secure and durable packaging solution.</p></p>
