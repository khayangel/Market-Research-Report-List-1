<p><p>The LED strip lights market is categorized into three types: 5050, 3528, and others. The 5050 LED strip lights are named after their dimensions of 5mm x 5mm and contain three LED chips per housing, providing a brighter and more colorful illumination. In contrast, the 3528 LED strip lights are named after their dimensions of 3.5mm x 2.8mm and contain only one LED chip per housing, producing less intense lighting. The "others" category includes LED strip lights that differ in dimensions, chip arrangement, or lighting capabilities from the aforementioned types.</p></p>
