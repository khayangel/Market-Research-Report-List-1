<p><p>The commercial bird feed market consists of two main types: staple and supplement. Staple bird feed is the primary source of nutrition for birds, providing them with essential nutrients and energy. It is designed to be the main component of a bird's diet. On the other hand, supplement bird feed is intended to enhance the diet of birds by providing additional nutrition and variety. It is used as a complementary feed alongside staple bird feed to meet specific dietary needs and preferences of different bird species.</p></p>
