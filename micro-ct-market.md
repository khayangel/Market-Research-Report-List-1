<p><p>The Micro-CT market can be divided into two main types: Structural Imaging and Functional Imaging. Structural Imaging focuses on capturing high-resolution images of the internal structures of objects, such as bones, tissues, and organs, providing valuable information for research and medical purposes. On the other hand, Functional Imaging focuses on acquiring real-time dynamic images, allowing researchers to observe and analyze the function and behavior of objects under various conditions. Both market types play a crucial role in advancing scientific research, medical diagnosis, and therapeutic development.</p></p>
