<p><p>The High Water Alarm market includes two types: AC Powered and DC Powered. AC Powered alarms are designed to be connected to an alternating current power source for their operation. They are suitable for locations where AC power is readily available. On the other hand, DC Powered alarms are designed to be connected to a direct current power source, such as batteries or solar panels. They are commonly used in remote or off-grid locations where there is no access to AC power. Both types serve the purpose of alerting users to rising water levels or potential flooding to prevent damage.</p></p>
