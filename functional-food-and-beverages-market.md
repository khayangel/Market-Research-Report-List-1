<p><p>Functional food and beverages are products that offer additional health benefits beyond their basic nutritional content. Some common examples of functional food and beverages include probiotic yogurt, fortified cereals, energy drinks with added vitamins, and herbal teas with antioxidant properties.</p><p>The functional food and beverages market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. Increasing consumer awareness about the importance of a healthy lifestyle, rising incidence of chronic diseases, and growing demand for convenient and nutritious food options are some of the factors driving the market growth. Additionally, advancements in food processing techniques and technologies have opened up new possibilities for the development of innovative functional products, further boosting the market.</p><p>One notable trend in the functional food and beverages market is the increasing popularity of plant-based functional products. Consumers are seeking plant-based alternatives to traditional animal-based functional foods, driven by concerns over environmental impact, animal welfare, and personal health. Companies are launching a range of plant-based functional products, including plant-based protein bars, non-dairy probiotic drinks, and fortified plant-based milks, to cater to this growing trend.</p><p>Another trend is the rising demand for personalized functional food and beverages. Consumers are increasingly seeking products tailored to their specific health needs and preferences. This has led to the development of personalized nutrition and functional food delivery services that provide customized meal plans and products based on individual requirements and goals.</p><p>Overall, the functional food and beverages market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
