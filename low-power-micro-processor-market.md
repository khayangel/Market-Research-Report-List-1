ARM-based MPUs, designed by ARM Holdings, are widely used in mobile devices, tablets, and embedded systems. These processors offer excellent power efficiency and are ideal for battery-powered devices. On the other hand, x86-based MPUs, developed by Intel and AMD, are commonly found in desktop computers, laptops, and servers.
