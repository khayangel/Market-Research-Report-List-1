<p><p>Compostable shopping bags come in three main types: organic waste, home compost, and industrial compost. Organic waste bags are designed to be used for collecting food scraps and other organic waste, and can be easily broken down in composting facilities. Home compost bags are meant for consumers who have their own composting system at home, enabling them to compost their shopping bags along with other organic waste. Industrial compost bags are typically used by businesses and industries, and are processed in commercial composting facilities where controlled conditions expedite decomposition.</p></p>
