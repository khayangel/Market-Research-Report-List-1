<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Inflight Internet System market is divided into three types: ATG (Air-to-Ground), Ka Band Satellite, and Ku Band Satellite. ATG utilizes ground-based stations to provide internet connectivity to inflight airplanes. Ka Band Satellite uses high-frequency signals from satellites positioned in space to offer fast and reliable internet access. Ku Band Satellite also relies on satellite technology but operates on a lower frequency range, catering to wider coverage areas. These systems aim to meet the increasing demand for in-flight connectivity and enhance passengers' browsing and communication experiences while traveling.</p></p>
