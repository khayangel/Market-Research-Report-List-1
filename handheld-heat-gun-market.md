<p><p>The handheld heat gun market consists of two main types: single temperature mode and dual temperature mode. This mode is ideal for users who have a specific heating requirement and do not need to switch between different temperature settings. On the other hand, the dual temperature mode offers two temperature settings, allowing users to switch between high and low temperatures according to their needs. This mode provides more versatility and flexibility for various heating applications.</p></p>
