<p><strong>The Human Leukocyte Antigen Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molecular Assay Technologies</li><li>Non-molecular Assay Technologies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Human Leukocyte Antigen (HLA) Testing market includes two main types of technologies: Molecular Assay Technologies and Non-molecular Assay Technologies. Molecular Assay Technologies involve analyzing the genetic material of an individual to identify HLA markers, and are mainly performed using techniques such as Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) and Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS). On the other hand, Non-molecular Assay Technologies focus on antibody-based testing, where the presence or absence of specific HLA proteins is determined using techniques like Flow Cytometry and Serology. Both types of technologies are used to assess compatibility for organ transplantation and study autoimmune diseases.</p></p>
