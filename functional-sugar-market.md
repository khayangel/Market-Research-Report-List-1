<p><p>Functional sugars refer to a range of sugar alternatives that offer various health benefits. Oligosaccharides are complex carbohydrates that promote gut health and aid digestion. Inulin is a soluble dietary fiber that helps regulate blood sugar levels and supports gut health. Sugar alcohols, like xylitol and erythritol, are low-calorie sweeteners that have minimal impact on blood sugar levels. The "Others" category includes alternative sweeteners such as stevia and monk fruit extract, which are natural, low-calorie options. These functional sugars cater to individuals looking for healthier sugar alternatives without compromising taste.</p></p>
