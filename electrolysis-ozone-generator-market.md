<p><p>An electrolysis ozone generator is a device that creates ozone gas through a process called electrolysis. It works by passing an electric current through water or air, which results in the splitting of oxygen molecules into individual atoms. These atoms then combine with other oxygen molecules to form ozone gas (O3). The generated ozone gas can be used for various applications such as water and air purification, odor removal, and disinfection.</p><p>The future outlook of the electrolysis ozone generator market is promising. With increasing concerns about air and water pollution, the demand for efficient and eco-friendly purification methods is growing.
