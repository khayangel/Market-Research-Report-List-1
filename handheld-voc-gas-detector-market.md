<p><p>The Handheld VOC Gas Detector market consists of two major types: the PID (Photoionization Detector) and the Metal-oxide Semiconductor detectors. PID detectors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, enabling the measurement of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in the atmosphere. On the other hand, Metal-oxide Semiconductor detectors employ a chemical reaction between the target gas and the metal-oxide material to produce an electrical signal, allowing for the detection of specific gases. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and monitoring air quality in various industries.</p></p>
