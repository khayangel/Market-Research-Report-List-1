<p><p>The polyester felt market can be categorized based on the thickness of the felt. The market is divided into three types: 0.5-5mm, 5-10mm, and above 10mm. Felt with a thickness of 0.5-5mm is commonly used for applications requiring a thin and flexible material, such as crafts and clothing accessories. Felt between 5-10mm thickness is suitable for heavy-duty purposes like industrial applications and sound insulation. Lastly, felt with a thickness above 10mm is primarily used for padding and cushioning, often found in mattress production or furniture upholstery. These different thickness categories cater to a variety of specific needs within the polyester felt market.</p></p>
