<p><p>Plasma surface treating equipment market can be categorized into two types based on pressure conditions: atmospheric pressure and low pressure/vacuum. Atmospheric pressure plasma surface treating equipment operates at normal or ambient pressure conditions, making it suitable for applications where high production rates are required. On the other hand, low pressure/vacuum plasma surface treating equipment operates at reduced pressure levels, making it ideal for delicate or sensitive materials that require precise treatment and enhanced surface activation. These different types cater to diverse industry needs and offer specific benefits in terms of efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
