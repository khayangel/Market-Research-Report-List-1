<p><p>The plunger valve market comprises two main types: manual plunger valves and electric plunger valves. Manual plunger valves are operated by hand, allowing users to control the flow of fluids or gases manually. On the other hand, electric plunger valves are automated valves that can be controlled remotely through an electrical signal. These valves are often used in industrial processes where precise and remote control of fluid or gas flow is required.
