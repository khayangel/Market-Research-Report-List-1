<p><p>A cystometry catheter is a medical device used for cystometry, which is a diagnostic procedure performed to measure the pressure inside the bladder. It helps in evaluating the bladder's capacity, detecting any abnormalities, and diagnosing conditions like urinary incontinence, overactive bladder, or neurogenic bladder. The catheter is usually inserted into the bladder through the urethra, and it contains pressure sensors that measure the pressure changes during filling and emptying of the bladder.</p><p>The cystometry catheter market is experiencing significant growth due to various factors. The increasing prevalence of urinary incontinence and overactive bladder, growing geriatric population, and rising awareness about bladder health are driving the demand for cystometry catheters.
