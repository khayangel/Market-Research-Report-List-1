<p><p>The radio modem market consists of various types based on the frequency band they operate in. License-free frequency radio modems operate on unlicensed bands, allowing for easy deployment but limited range. UHF (Ultra High Frequency) modems provide longer range and better penetration through obstacles. Wi-Fi modems use the popular wireless communication standard for short-range high-speed data transfer. VHF (Very High Frequency) modems offer excellent coverage but lower data rates. Each of these market types caters to different needs, offering varying trade-offs between range, data speed, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
