<p><ul><li>Bolt-On Type Rubber Track Pads</li><li>Clip-On Type Rubber Track Pads</li><li>Chain-On Type Rubber Track Pads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber track pads are used on excavators to protect the surface they operate on and enhance traction. There are three main types of rubber track pads available in the market. Bolt-On type rubber track pads require drilling and bolting onto the steel track shoes. Clip-On type rubber track pads are easy to install and clip onto the existing steel track shoes. Chain-On type rubber track pads have a steel chain that wraps around the steel track shoes and is secured with a fastening system. Each type offers different benefits and suitability for specific excavation needs.</p></p>
