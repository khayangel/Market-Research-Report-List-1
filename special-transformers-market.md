<p><p>Special Transformers Market Types- Liquid Filled, Dry-type:</p><p>Liquid-filled transformers refer to those that contain an insulating liquid, typically oil, which helps in dissipating heat and minimizing electric losses. These transformers are widely used in industrial and utility settings due to their efficient cooling properties and high power capacity.</p><p>On the other hand, dry-type transformers do not require any liquid for cooling and insulation purposes. They are designed with advanced insulation materials that enable them to withstand high temperatures. Dry-type transformers are preferred in residential, commercial, and indoor applications due to their reduced fire risk, low maintenance requirements, and environmental friendliness.</p></p>
