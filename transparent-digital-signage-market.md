<p><p>The transparent digital signage market encompasses various types, including LED Type, OLED Type, and Others. LED Type consists of See-through Type and Film Type solutions, both utilizing LED technology to create transparent displays. See-through Type uses LED panels to form a clear visual, making objects behind the display visible. Film Type, on the other hand, employs LED film sheets that can be attached to glass surfaces, turning them into digital screens. OLED Type involves using OLED technology for transparent displays, offering high-resolution and vibrant visuals. Lastly, the Others category includes alternative technologies beyond LED and OLED for transparent digital signage.</p></p>
