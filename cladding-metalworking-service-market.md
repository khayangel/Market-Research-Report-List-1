<p><p>Cladding service in metalworking includes various techniques such as Roll Bonding, Explosive Welding, and Laser Cladding. Roll bonding involves bonding two metal sheets together through pressure and heat. Explosive welding is a process where two metals are forced together under high pressure, creating a strong bond. Laser cladding uses a laser beam to melt and fuse a layer of metal onto a base material, providing enhanced properties. These techniques offer different options for cladding in metalworking processes, allowing for improved functionality and durability of metal products.</p></p>
