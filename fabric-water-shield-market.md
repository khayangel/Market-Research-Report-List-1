<p><p>Fabric Water Shield is a type of protective coating applied to various fabrics to make them water-resistant. There are two main types of Fabric Water Shield on the market: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based fabric water shields use water as the primary solvent to disperse the active chemicals, making them more environmentally friendly and less harmful. On the other hand, solvent-based fabric water shields use organic solvents as the dispersing medium, making them more durable and longer-lasting. Both types offer effective water protection for fabrics, but water-based shields are preferred for their eco-friendliness while solvent-based shields are renowned for their durability.</p></p>
