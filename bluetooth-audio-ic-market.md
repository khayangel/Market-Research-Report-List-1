<p><p>The Bluetooth Audio IC market can be segmented into two types: Ordinary Bluetooth Audio Chip and Audio SoC market. An ordinary Bluetooth Audio Chip serves the purpose of wireless audio transmission, typically used in devices like headphones and speakers. On the other hand, the Audio SoC (System on Chip) market includes integrated circuits that not only perform audio transmission but also incorporate additional functionalities like audio decoding and signal processing. These advanced chips are commonly used in smart devices such as smartphones, tablets, and smart speakers, offering enhanced audio performance and capabilities.</p></p>
