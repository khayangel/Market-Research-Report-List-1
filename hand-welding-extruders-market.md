<p><ul><li>Contact Welding</li><li>Hot Gas Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hand welding extruders are used in various industries for welding thermoplastic materials. Contact welding is a type of hand welding extruder process where two surfaces are brought in contact with each other, and heat is applied to melt the thermoplastic materials. Hot gas welding, on the other hand, involves heating the thermoplastic materials using a stream of hot air or gas, which melts the surfaces and fuses them together. Both contact welding and hot gas welding are widely used in applications such as plastic fabrication, automotive repair, and plumbing.</p></p>
