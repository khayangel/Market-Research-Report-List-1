<p><p>Trailer fenders come in two main types: half fenders and full fenders. Half fenders are shorter in length and are typically installed at the front of the trailer. They provide protection against road debris and help enhance the trailer's overall appearance. On the other hand, full fenders are longer and cover the entire length of the trailer wheels. They offer greater protection from road hazards and serve to prevent water and mud splashing onto the trailer, reducing damage and increasing safety during transportation.</p></p>
