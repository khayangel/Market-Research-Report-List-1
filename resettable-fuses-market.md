<p><p>Resettable fuses are a type of electrical protection device that can automatically recover from overload conditions. Polymer type resettable fuses utilize conductive polymer material to provide protection against overcurrent events. These fuses offer quick response times, low resistance, and compact size, making them suitable for various applications. On the other hand, ceramic type resettable fuses utilize a ceramic disc instead of conductive polymer material to provide overload protection. These fuses are known for their high current ratings, superior thermal performance, and wide operating temperature range, making them ideal for industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
