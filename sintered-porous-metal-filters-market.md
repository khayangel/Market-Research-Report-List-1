<p><ul><li>Low Porosity (Below 30％)</li><li>Medium Porosity (30～60％)</li><li>High Porosity (Above 60％)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sintered porous metal filters are used in various industries to separate solids from liquids or gases. They come in three types based on porosity levels. Low porosity filters have less than 30% pore volume, providing better particle retention and resistance to backwash. Medium porosity filters have a pore volume between 30% and 60%, offering balanced filtration efficiency and flow rates. High porosity filters with over 60% pore volume allow for higher flow rates but lower particle retention. Choosing the right porosity level depends on specific filtration requirements and system parameters.</p></p>
