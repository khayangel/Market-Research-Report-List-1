<p><strong>The Spray Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray humidifiers are divided into two main types: high pressure and low pressure. High pressure spray humidifiers use a pump that forcefully sprays water droplets into the air, resulting in a fine mist. These humidifiers are suitable for large commercial or industrial spaces that require precise control over humidity levels. On the other hand, low pressure spray humidifiers release water droplets into the air through nozzles using natural air pressure. These types are commonly used in residential settings or small commercial spaces where humidity control is not as critical.</p></p>
