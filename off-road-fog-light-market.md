<p><p>The off-road fog light market consists of two types: round lights and light bars. Round lights are designed in a circular shape and emit a focused beam of light. They are commonly mounted on the front bumper or roof rack of off-road vehicles. On the other hand, light bars are long, rectangular-shaped lights that provide a wider and more extensive illumination. They are often installed on the roof or front grille of off-road vehicles, offering increased visibility in challenging terrains or adverse weather conditions.</p></p>
