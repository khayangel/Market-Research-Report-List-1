<p><p>Mechanical wrist watches are timepieces that rely on mechanical mechanisms to keep time rather than relying on electronic components. They use a mainspring as the primary power source, which is wound either manually or automatically through the natural motion of the wearer's wrist.</p><p>The mechanical wrist watches market has witnessed significant growth over the years. The demand for these watches has been driven by factors such as their intricate craftsmanship, high-quality materials, and timeless appeal.
