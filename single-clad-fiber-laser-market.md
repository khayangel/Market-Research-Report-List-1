<p><strong>The Single-Clad Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-Clad Fiber Lasers are classified into two main market types, Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser and Pulsed Fiber Laser. Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers emit a continuous beam of laser light, making them ideal for applications requiring steady power output. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit short bursts of high-intensity laser light, making them suitable for applications where precision and control over pulse duration are needed. Both types of fiber lasers have unique advantages and are utilized in various industrial, medical, and scientific applications.</p></p>
