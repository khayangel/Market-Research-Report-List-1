This, in turn, is driving the demand for on-grid string inverters.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have led to the development of more efficient and reliable on-grid string inverters. Manufacturers are focusing on improving the efficiency of these inverters, reducing their size and weight, and enhancing their durability and performance. These technological advancements are attracting more consumers and driving market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a growing preference for hybrid on-grid string inverters that can also function as energy storage systems.
