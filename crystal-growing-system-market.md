<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Crystal Growing System market includes two main types: the Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace and the Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace. The CZ Method Furnace involves the growth of crystals by slowly pulling a seed crystal from a melt. This method is commonly used for producing high-quality single crystals. On the other hand, the FZ Method Furnace utilizes a focused light source to melt a small zone of a polycrystalline rod and then grow a single crystal by pulling it from the melt. Both methods are widely used in various industries for the production of crystals with specific properties.</p></p>
